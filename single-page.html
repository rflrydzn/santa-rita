Santa Rita de Cascia Parish

Discover the beauty and history of Santa Rita de Cascia Parish

Welcome to Santa Rita de Cascia Parish, a sanctuary of spirituality and cultural heritage. Nestled in the heart of Pampanga, Philippines, our parish is more than just a religious institution; it's a testament to centuries of faith, tradition, and community. Our iconic church stands as a beacon of history, its architecture telling the tales of generations that have gathered within its walls. The stunning blend of Spanish and Filipino influences makes it a unique and cherished landmark in the region. As you step into the hallowed grounds, feel the tranquility that permeates the air. Attend our spiritual events, participate in community activities, and witness the unwavering spirit of our parishioners.
