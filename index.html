SANTA RITA


Santa Rita, Pampanga, is a captivating destination offering a blend of history, nature, and culture. Its historic Santa Rita de Cascia Parish Church and picturesque Mount Arayat stand out as must-see attractions. Enjoy vibrant festivals, local markets, and delicious cuisine, making it an enriching and diverse destination for all travelers.

SANTA RITA DE CASIA


The Santa Rita de Casia Parish Church is a beautiful and historically significant Roman Catholic church, known for its intricate baroque design. Dedicated to Saint Rita of Cascia, the patron saint of impossible causes, it's a popular pilgrimage site.
