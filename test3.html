SANTA RITA


Santa Rita, Pampanga, is a captivating destination offering a blend of history, nature, and culture. Its historic Santa Rita de Cascia Parish Church and picturesque Mount Arayat stand out as must-see attractions. Enjoy vibrant festivals, local markets, and delicious cuisine, making it an enriching and diverse destination for all travelers.

Santa Rita, Pampanga, is a gastronomic haven where you can savor Kapampangan classics like Sisig and Tibok-Tibok, offering a delightful taste of local culture and heritage. Don't miss the chance to explore the flavors of Santa Rita and indulge in a unique culinary experience during your visit.

Recreation in Santa Rita, Pampanga, offers a delightful array of activities for those seeking leisure and entertainment. Enjoy a leisurely day by the serene Santa Rita Dam, perfect for picnics, fishing, or simply unwinding amidst picturesque surroundings. Golf enthusiasts can tee off at the nearby Mimosa Golf and Country Club, offering lush greens and stunning views of Mount Arayat.

Santa Rita, Pampanga, is a place where history and spirituality converge, with several remarkable churches that are not to be missed. The Santa Rita de Cascia Parish Church is the town's centerpiece, with its grand baroque architecture and a rich history dating back to Spanish colonial times.

SANTA RITA DE CASIA


The Santa Rita de Casia Parish Church is a beautiful and historically significant Roman Catholic church, known for its intricate baroque design. Dedicated to Saint Rita of Cascia, the patron saint of impossible causes, it's a popular pilgrimage site.
